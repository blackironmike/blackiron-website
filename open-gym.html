What Is Open Gym?

Open Gym gives you full access to the Black Iron Athletics facility 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Whether you prefer training at 5 AM before work, during your lunch break, or at midnight when the rest of the world is asleep, the gym is yours. It's the ultimate flexibility for athletes who refuse to let a busy schedule stand between them and their goals.

This program is perfect for self-motivated athletes who want the freedom to train on their own terms. You'll have access to everything in the facility -- barbells, dumbbells, squat racks, rigs, cardio machines, and more. Entry is simple and secure with key fob or code access, and the facility is climate-controlled, clean, and well-maintained every single day.

Can't make it to a scheduled class? No problem. Open Gym members can also follow Black Iron's structured programming on their own schedule. You get the benefit of expert-designed workouts with the flexibility to complete them whenever it works for you.

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about 24/7 Open Gym access.

How do I access the gym after hours?

Open Gym members receive a secure key fob and access code with their membership. Simply use your key fob or code at the door to enter the facility any time, day or night.

Is the gym monitored?

Yes. We have security cameras throughout the facility for the safety of all our members. Your security is a top priority, and the monitoring system runs 24/7.

Can I bring a friend?

For safety and liability purposes, only active members can access the facility during open gym hours. If your friend is interested in joining, have them book a free consultation and we'll get them set up.

What if I need help with equipment?

Our coaches are available during scheduled class hours to help with form, programming questions, or equipment guidance. We also provide programming guides so you always know what to do during your open gym sessions.

Is there a separate open gym area?

No -- and that's the best part. You have access to the full facility, including all equipment, racks, rigs, and floor space. The entire gym is yours to use.

Can I combine open gym with group classes?

Absolutely. Our Unlimited + Open Gym membership includes both unlimited group classes and full 24/7 open gym access. It's the best of both worlds for athletes who want coaching and flexibility.
