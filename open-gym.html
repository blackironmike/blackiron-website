What Is Open Gym?

Open Gym gives you full access to the Black Iron Athletics facility 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. It's the ultimate flexibility for athletes who refuse to let a busy schedule stand between them and their goals. Entry is simple and secure with key fob access, and the facility is climate-controlled and well-maintained. You can also follow our structured programming on your own schedule.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about 24/7 Open Gym access.

How do I access the gym after hours?

Open Gym members receive a secure key fob and access code with their membership. Simply use your key fob or code at the door to enter the facility any time, day or night.

Can I bring a friend?

For safety and liability purposes, only active members can access the facility during open gym hours. If your friend is interested in joining, have them book a free consultation and we'll get them set up.

Can I combine open gym with group classes?

Absolutely. Our Unlimited + Open Gym membership includes both unlimited group classes and full 24/7 open gym access. It's the best of both worlds for athletes who want coaching and flexibility.
