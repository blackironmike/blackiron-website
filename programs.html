Our Programs

From coach-led group classes to 1-on-1 personal training, nutrition coaching, and 24/7 open gym access -- we have everything you need to reach your goals.

Coach-Led Classes

Group Classes

Coach-led group classes are the heart of Black Iron Athletics. After over a decade as a CrossFit affiliate, we evolved our programming into something better -- taking the best elements of functional fitness and combining them with smarter structure, more predictable results, and less wear and tear on the body. Every class is led by a certified coach, scales to every fitness level, and is designed to build real-world strength, endurance, and community. If you are looking for a CrossFit alternative in Frisco that delivers the same intensity and camaraderie without the affiliation, you have found it.

Strength & Conditioning

Our mainstay program. Built from over a decade of CrossFit coaching experience with a modern approach. Each 60-minute session includes functional strength work, HIIT-style metabolic conditioning (metcons), and skill development. Think AMRAPs, EMOMs, and workout-of-the-day style programming -- all with smarter structure and expert coaching.

Weightlifting

Olympic weightlifting focused on improving your lifts and maximizing your potential with a barbell. Whether you're learning the clean & jerk for the first time or chasing a new PR, this class is built for progress.

Hyrox

Focused on getting you race day ready. A blend of running and functional fitness stations designed to prepare you for Hyrox competition or just push your endurance to the next level.

"All movement is good movement. We offer different classes because people have different passions — but the mission never changes: sweat together, grow together, and enjoy the process."
