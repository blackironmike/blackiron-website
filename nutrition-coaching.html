The Program

What Is Nutrition Coaching?

This is not a fad diet. It is not a restrictive meal plan that leaves you hungry, frustrated, and right back where you started. Nutrition coaching at Black Iron Athletics is a sustainable, personalized approach to fueling your body the right way -- built around your life, your preferences, and your goals.

You will work 1-on-1 with a dedicated coach who takes the time to understand how you eat, how you train, and what has or has not worked for you in the past. From there, they build a nutrition strategy designed specifically for you. Whether that means tracking macros, following an intuitive eating approach, or something in between, we meet you where you are and build from there.

The goal is simple: help you develop a relationship with food that supports your training, your health, and your long-term well-being. No guilt. No gimmicks. Just a clear plan, consistent support, and real results that last.

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our nutrition coaching program

Do I have to follow a strict diet?

No. We take a flexible approach that fits your lifestyle. There are no banned foods and no rigid meal schedules. Your coach will help you build a plan that works with how you actually live, not against it.

Can I do nutrition coaching without training at the gym?

Yes. Nutrition coaching is available as a standalone service or combined with any of our training memberships. Whether you train with us or somewhere else, we can help you dial in your nutrition.

How often do we check in?

Check-ins happen weekly or bi-weekly depending on your plan and your needs. Between check-ins, you have direct access to your coach via text and email for any questions that come up.

Will I have to give up foods I love?

No. We believe in balance and moderation, not restriction. Your coach will teach you how to enjoy the foods you love while still making progress toward your goals. Sustainability is the priority.

How soon will I see results?

Most clients begin to see and feel changes within 2-4 weeks. Energy levels improve, clothes fit differently, and training performance picks up. Long-term body composition changes build from there with consistency.

Do you work with dietary restrictions?

Yes. We accommodate all dietary needs and restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, food allergies, and more. Your plan is built around what works for your body.
