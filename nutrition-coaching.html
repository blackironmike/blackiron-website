What Is Nutrition Coaching?

Nutrition coaching at Black Iron Athletics is a sustainable, personalized approach to fueling your body the right way. You'll work 1-on-1 with a dedicated coach who builds a nutrition strategy designed specifically for you -- whether that means tracking macros, intuitive eating, or something in between. No guilt, no gimmicks, just a clear plan and real results that last.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our nutrition coaching program

Do I have to follow a strict diet?

No. We take a flexible approach that fits your lifestyle. There are no banned foods and no rigid meal schedules. Your coach will help you build a plan that works with how you actually live, not against it.

Can I do nutrition coaching without training at the gym?

Yes. Nutrition coaching is available as a standalone service or combined with any of our training memberships. Whether you train with us or somewhere else, we can help you dial in your nutrition.

Do you work with dietary restrictions?

Yes. We accommodate all dietary needs and restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, food allergies, and more. Your plan is built around what works for your body.
