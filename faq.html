Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about training at Black Iron Athletics.

Getting Started Classes Membership Facility Nutrition

Getting Started

Is Black Iron Athletics beginner-friendly?

Yes, all fitness levels are welcome. Our coaches scale every workout to your ability so you can train safely and effectively from day one.

Do I need to be in shape before I start?

No. We meet you where you are. Many of our members started with zero fitness experience. You just have to be willing to show up.

What's the first step to joining?

Book a free consultation. We'll talk about your goals, give you a tour, and recommend the best program for you.

Can I try a class before committing?

Yes! Book a free consultation and we'll get you into a class to experience it firsthand. No pressure, no commitment required.

What should I wear to my first class?

Athletic clothes and shoes you can move in. Cross-training shoes work best. We provide everything else.

Classes & Training

What types of classes do you offer?

Group training classes that combine strength, conditioning, and functional movement. We also offer personal training and nutrition coaching.

How long are classes?

About 60 minutes including warm-up, skill/strength work, conditioning, and cool-down.

How many people are in each class?

We keep class sizes small so coaches can give everyone personal attention and ensure safe, effective training.

What if I can't do a certain movement?

Every movement has a modification. Our coaches will scale exercises to your ability level -- no one gets left behind.

How often should I work out?

We recommend 3-5 times per week depending on your goals, but you can start with whatever frequency fits your schedule.

Membership & Pricing

What membership options do you offer?

Standard (3x/week), Unlimited + Open Gym, Personal Training, and Open Gym Only. Visit our Getting Started page for details.

Are there contracts or long-term commitments?

We offer month-to-month memberships. No long-term contracts required.

Do you offer a free trial?

We offer a free consultation that includes experiencing a class. Book yours today.

What's included in my membership?

Depends on your plan. All memberships include expert coaching, structured programming, community events, and monthly body composition scans.

Do you offer military or first responder discounts?

Yes! As a veteran-owned gym, we proudly support those who serve. Ask us about our military and first responder pricing.

Facility & Access

Where are you located?

279 Main St #122, Frisco, TX 75036 -- right in the heart of Frisco.

What are your hours?

Group classes run throughout the day -- check our schedule page. Open Gym members have 24/7 access.

Is the gym climate controlled?

Yes. AC in summer, heat in winter. You'll train in comfort year-round.

Do you have parking?

Yes, free parking is available right outside our facility.

What equipment do you have?

Full range: barbells, dumbbells, kettlebells, squat racks, rigs, rowers, bikes, ski ergs, and more.

Nutrition & Results

Do you offer nutrition coaching?

Yes. Our nutrition coaching program includes personalized meal planning, macro guidance, regular check-ins, and ongoing support.

Can I do nutrition coaching without a gym membership?

Yes, nutrition coaching is available as a standalone service.

How soon will I see results?

Most members notice improvements in energy and confidence within 2 weeks. Visible body composition changes typically come within 4-8 weeks with consistent training and nutrition.

Do you track progress?

Yes. We use Wodify to track workouts and PRs, and offer monthly body composition scans to measure real progress.

What makes Black Iron different from other gyms?

We're a veteran-owned, coach-led facility focused on community and results. Every class is led by an expert coach. We don't just give you access -- we give you guidance, accountability, and a family that wants to see you succeed.
