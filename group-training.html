The Experience

What Is Group Training?

Group training at Black Iron Athletics is where real transformation happens. Every class is led by a certified coach who guides you through a structured workout designed to build functional strength, improve conditioning, and develop movement skills that translate to everyday life. Whether you're picking up your kids, playing a sport, or just want to feel stronger and more capable, our programming is built with purpose.

Our classes combine elements of strength training, metabolic conditioning, and gymnastic-style movements in a format that's challenging, efficient, and scalable for every fitness level. You'll never be left guessing what to do or wondering if you're doing it right. Your coach is there every step of the way, correcting form, offering modifications, and pushing you to be better than yesterday.

What makes our group training different is the community. You're not just another face in a crowded gym. You train alongside people who know your name, celebrate your wins, and hold you accountable. The energy of training together pushes you further than you'd ever go on your own, and the friendships you build here extend far beyond the gym walls.

Warm-Up

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be in shape to start?

Not at all. Our group training classes welcome all fitness levels, from complete beginners to seasoned athletes. Every workout is scalable, meaning your coach will adjust movements, weights, and intensity to match where you are right now.

How many people are in each class?

We keep our class sizes intentionally small so our coaches can give you personal attention. You'll never feel lost in a crowd. Every member gets real coaching, form corrections, and encouragement throughout the entire session.

What should I bring?

Just bring a water bottle, a towel, and a pair of athletic shoes with a flat sole. We provide all the equipment you need. Show up ready to work and we'll handle the rest.

How often should I come?

We recommend 3 to 5 sessions per week for the best results, but your schedule is flexible. Even 3 days a week produces incredible changes when you're consistent. Your coach can help you find the right frequency for your goals.

Can I try a class before committing?

Yes! The first step is to book a free consultation where we'll learn about your goals and you'll get to experience what we do firsthand. There's no pressure and no obligation. We want you to feel confident that this is the right fit.

What if I can't do a movement?

Our coaches provide modifications for every single movement. If you can't do a pull-up, we'll scale it. If a certain movement aggravates an old injury, we'll find an alternative. No one gets left behind, and no one gets hurt.
