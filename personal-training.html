The Experience

What Is Personal Training?

Personal training at Black Iron Athletics is dedicated 1-on-1 time with a certified coach who designs everything around YOU. Every session, every rep, every program detail is built specifically for your body, your goals, and your timeline. This isn't a generic plan pulled from the internet -- it's expert coaching with your name on it.

Personal training is ideal for people with specific goals like weight loss, injury rehabilitation, or competition preparation. It's also the perfect fit for anyone who wants undivided attention from their coach, prefers a private training environment, or simply wants to accelerate their results with a more focused approach.

Your programs are customized, progressive, and data-driven. We track everything -- your lifts, your body composition, your movement quality -- so that every decision we make is backed by real numbers, not guesswork. You'll always know where you stand and exactly what comes next.

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I train?

Most personal training clients train 2-4 times per week depending on their goals, schedule, and recovery needs. During your consultation, we'll recommend the ideal frequency for your situation.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

Personal training is the perfect starting point. We meet you exactly where you are -- no experience required. Your coach will teach you proper form, build your confidence, and progress you at the right pace.

How long are sessions?

Sessions are typically 60 minutes. That gives us enough time for a proper warm-up, your training session, and a cool-down -- all with expert coaching throughout.

Can I combine personal training with group classes?

Absolutely. Many of our members do both. Personal training builds your individual strengths while group classes add community, variety, and extra conditioning.

Do you offer nutrition coaching with personal training?

Yes, nutrition guidance is included as part of your personal training program. Your coach will help you build sustainable eating habits that support your training and accelerate your results.

What results can I expect?

Results depend on your goals and consistency, but most clients see significant progress within 6-8 weeks. Whether it's weight loss, strength gains, or improved movement quality, we track everything so you can see your transformation in real time.
