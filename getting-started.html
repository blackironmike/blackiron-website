What to Expect

Here's what happens after you book your free consultation

Free Consultation

We sit down and talk about your goals, any concerns you have, and what you want to achieve. This is a judgment-free conversation where we get to know you and you get to know us.

Custom Plan Created

Based on your goals and fitness level, we'll build a personalized training plan. Whether you need one-on-one coaching to build confidence or you're ready to jump into group classes, we'll map out the right path for you.

Start Training

If you're new, you'll work one-on-one with a coach to learn proper form and build confidence. If you have experience, you'll join our group classes with full support from our coaching team.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started

Is it beginner friendly?

Absolutely. We have one-on-one onboarding to make sure you feel confident and ready to jump into the program.

Do I have to be in shape to join?

Not at all. You just have to be willing to move. Everyone starts somewhere, and we're here to meet you where you are.

What if I have an old injury?

We work with injuries and limitations all the time. Just talk to your coach and we're happy to accommodate. You won't be a burden, you'll be supported.

How long until I see results?

Most members notice changes in how they feel within 2 weeks. Visible physical changes typically show up in 4-6 weeks with consistent training.

What if I've tried gyms before and quit?

The difference here is community and accountability. Our coaches know your name, track your progress, and our members support each other.

Can I try a class before I commit?

Your free consultation includes the opportunity to experience what we do before making any decisions.

What's your cancellation policy?

We operate on month-to-month memberships. No long-term contracts. If you need to pause or cancel, just give us notice.

How is this different from a regular gym?

You get expert coaching, structured programming designed for results, and a community that pushes you to show up. You're not just renting equipment access.
