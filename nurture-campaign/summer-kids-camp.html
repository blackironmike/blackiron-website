Summer Athletics Camp

Six weeks of real coaching for ages 8–12. Starting June 1st.

 

Summer is coming, and we know what that means — kids are home.

Black Iron is hosting a six-week athletics camp for ages 8–12, starting June 1st. The goal: keep your kids active, build real athletic habits, and prep them for whatever comes next — whether that’s a fall sport, a school team, or just moving better through life.

Coach Evan and Coach Emily will be running the program, and they’re ready to help your young athletes grow.

The Focus

▶ Proper form and technique. Learn the right way from the start.
▶ Strength and conditioning. Build a base that carries over to any sport.
▶ Agility and plyometrics. Move faster, react quicker.
▶ Most of all — fun. This is still summer. They’ll love showing up.

The Mission

Develop stronger, more capable athletes.
