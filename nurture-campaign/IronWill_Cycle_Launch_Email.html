What Is Iron Will?

Iron Will is a 9-week strength cycle running from April 20 through June 26. Where The Forge focused on control and position, this one is all about speed, explosiveness, and power.

The three main lifts:

▶  Conventional Deadlift (3 Rep Max test)
▶  Shoulder Press (1 Rep Max test)
▶  Hang Power Clean (2 Rep Max test)

The cycle is built around cluster sets. If you haven't seen these before, here's the short version: a cluster set is a working set broken into mini-sets with about 15 seconds of rest between them, same weight throughout. It lets you train heavier without grinding through ugly reps. Definitely going to be cool.

Here's how the 9 weeks break down:

▶  Week 0 (this week): Introduction
▶  Week 1: Test your starting maxes
▶  Weeks 2 through 4: Build phase
▶  Week 5 (Memorial Day): Deload
▶  Weeks 6 through 8: Final build
▶  Week 9: Retest. See how far you've come.

We'll also have a few conditioning benchmarks mixed in for good measure. You'll find out what those are next week.

So what is Xenon?

It's a brand new competition format being called the "Decathlon of Fitness." Think of it as a mix between traditional functional fitness competition and Hyrox, with 10 standardized events that never change. The events are well rounded across weightlifting, gymnastics, and conditioning. The goal is to standardize the sport of fitness with a modern approach.

People from Xenon HQ will be at the gym on Saturday talking about what they're building and what's coming next. The very first official Xenon event ever will be right here in Frisco in June, so this is a great chance to learn more and get in early.
