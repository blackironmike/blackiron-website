What Is Xenon?

Xenon is a brand new competition format being called the "Decathlon of Fitness." Think of it as a mix between traditional functional fitness competition and Hyrox, with 10 standardized events that never change. The events are well rounded across weightlifting, gymnastics, and conditioning. The goal is to standardize the sport of fitness with a modern approach.

People from Xenon HQ will be at the gym on Saturday talking about what they're building and what's coming next. The very first official Xenon event ever will be right here in Frisco in June, so this is a great chance to learn more and get in early.
