They land on your main site or directly on /blueprint.Step 2: They read the Landing Page — see the 4-phase overview, the value proposition, and the Founding Member price of $99/year.Step 3: They explore the free pages: Philosophy and Calendar. They start to understand the system and want the full details.Step 4: They click “Get the Full Blueprint” — MemberSpace popup appears. They enter their email, create a password, and pay $99 through Stripe.Step 5: Instant access to all 7 premium pages. They start with “How to Use This System,” run the macro calculator three times, and they’re off.PATH 2: Existing Black Iron MemberStep 1: You announce the Blueprint in the gym, on your email list, or in your member community.Step 2: They go directly to /blueprint and sign up at the Founding Member rate (consider offering your current gym members a discount or first-access window).Step 3: They run the calculator using their latest InBody scan data for maximum accuracy, giving them an edge over non-members who are estimating.PATH 3: Free User Who Doesn’t Convert (Yet)Step 1: They read the free pages, maybe run the macro calculator, but don’t buy.Step 2: They see premium page links throughout the free content (teaser text, locked section previews). Every phase transition (4x/year), you post content about the Blueprint that reminds them it exists.Step 3: They convert later — often when a specific phase is about to start and your content creates urgency.This is why the free tier matters. It’s not charity — it’s a slow-burn conversion engine.PAGE-BY-PAGE BUILD GUIDEPAGE 1: LANDING PAGE (Sales Page)/blueprint   [ FREE ]This is your sales page. Follow these steps after you’ve signed up at memberspace.com.STEP 1: Install the MemberSpace ScriptMemberSpace gives you a small JavaScript snippet during setup. Add it to the <head> section of every page on your site, or at minimum, every page under /blueprint/. If you’re using a shared header template across your site, add it there once and it covers everything.STEP 2: Create Your PlanIn MemberSpace, create one plan:Plan Name: The 4-Phase Nutrition BlueprintPrice: $99 (set as a recurring annual payment)Payment Type: Recurring — $99/year (this auto-renews, which is what you want for recurring revenue)Description: “Year-round nutrition periodization system. Includes all 4 phase guides, macro calculator integration, supplement protocols, hydration guide, and the Decade Roadmap.”STEP 3: Create a “Space” (Content Group)In MemberSpace, a “Space” is a group of protected pages. Create one Space called “Blueprint Premium” and add all 7 premium page URLs:/blueprint/how-to-use/blueprint/phase-1-maintain/blueprint/phase-2-cut/blueprint/phase-3-maintain/blueprint/phase-4-build/blueprint/supplements/blueprint/decadeThen connect the “4-Phase Nutrition Blueprint” plan to this Space. Now anyone who joins that plan gets access to all 7 pages at once.STEP 4: Set Your Redirect URLsPost-Signup Redirect: /blueprint/how-to-use (this is the first page new members should see)Post-Login Redirect: /blueprint/how-to-use (returning members land here when they log in)Non-Member Redirect: /blueprint (send non-members to the landing/sales page if they try to access a premium URL directly)STEP 5: Add Signup/Login ButtonsMemberSpace provides you with two link types:Signup Link: Use this for all “Get the Blueprint” and “Unlock All Phases” CTAs on your free pages. Clicking this opens the MemberSpace signup popup.Login Link: Add a “Member Login” button to your site’s navigation bar (top right, next to your existing “Book Free Consultation” button). This lets returning members sign in.STEP 6: Connect StripeMemberSpace walks you through Stripe setup during onboarding. Stripe handles all payment processing. Payments hit your Stripe account (minus Stripe’s 2.9% + $0.30 and MemberSpace’s 5% transaction fee). You can view all members, payments, and cancellations in both the MemberSpace dashboard and your Stripe dashboard.STEP 7: Test the Full FlowBefore you announce anything, test every step yourself:☐  Visit /blueprint as a logged-out user — can you see the landing page?☐  Click a premium page link — does MemberSpace block it and show the signup popup?☐  Complete a test signup (MemberSpace has test mode) — does payment process?☐  After signup, are you redirected to /blueprint/how-to-use?☐  Can you navigate to all 7 premium pages without being blocked?☐  Log out and try accessing a premium page — are you redirected to /blueprint?☐  Test the login button — can you log back in and access premium content?NAVIGATION & SITE INTEGRATIONMAIN SITE NAVIGATIONAdd the Blueprint to your existing site nav.
