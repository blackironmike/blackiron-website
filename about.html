About Us

Our Mission

Why We Exist

Who We Are

We help everyday people transform their health by providing expert coaching, smart programming, and genuine accountability so they can become happier, healthier athletes who live with purpose and vitality.

At Black Iron Athletics, getting fit isn't about quick fixes or empty promises. It's about building a body that serves you for life. A body that lets you chase your kids, pursue your goals, and show up with confidence every single day.

Our Story

Black Iron Athletics was founded in 2013 with a simple belief: movement is medicine, and everyone deserves access to world-class training in a supportive environment.

As a veteran-owned business, we built this gym on the principles of discipline, integrity, and service. We don't believe in shortcuts or gimmicks. We believe in hard work, accountability, and showing up for each other every single day.

Movement is medicine for both the body and the mind. We understand that mental health can be even more challenging than the physical side of things, which is why we don't expect you to do this alone. We're here with you every step of the way, and so are the athletes training right next to you.

Over the past 12+ years, we've helped over 3,000 people transform their lives. From complete beginners stepping into a gym for the first time to experienced athletes chasing new PRs, we've guided them all with the same commitment: meet people where they are and help them become who they're meant to be.

What We Believe

Movement Is Medicine

Your health isn't selfish. It's service. When you prioritize your fitness, you show up stronger for your family, your work, and your community.

Quality of Life Over Ego

Yes, looking good and feeling confident matters. But it's not about chasing someone else's idea of perfect. It's about building real strength, preventing injury, and creating a body that serves you well for decades. When you feel strong and capable, confidence follows naturally.

Community and Accountability

You don't have to figure this out on your own. Our coaches and your fellow athletes are here to support you, push you, and celebrate every win along the way.

Mental Health Matters

We know the mental battle can be harder than the physical one. That's why we've built a community that supports the whole person, not just the workout.
