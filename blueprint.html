The Science

Why We Cycle

Your metabolism adapts. If you diet forever, your body fights back â€” slowing your metabolism, spiking hunger hormones, and burning muscle for fuel. Periodized nutrition keeps your metabolism healthy by cycling through strategic phases.

Baseline

Months 1-3

Establish your true maintenance calories. Reset hunger signals, stabilize hormones, and build the habits that make everything else work.

Cut

Months 4-6

Strategic calorie deficit to shed body fat while preserving the muscle you've built. Time-limited so your metabolism doesn't adapt.

Maintain

Months 7-9

Lock in your new body composition. Let hormones recover, metabolic rate normalize, and your body accept this as the new baseline.

Build

Months 10-12

Controlled surplus to add lean muscle. More muscle means higher metabolism, which means your next cut is even more effective.

Chronic Dieting

Endless restriction. Metabolism slows. Muscle wastes. Hormones crash. You lose the same 10 lbs every January and gain back 15 by summer.

4-Phase Cycling

Strategic phases. Metabolism stays healthy. Muscle grows. Each year you start from a better place than the last. Progress compounds.

Blueprint FAQs

Do I need to be a Black Iron Athletics member?

No. The Blueprint works for anyone, anywhere. It's built around the free Black Iron Macro Calculator, which is available online to everyone. If you're a local BIA member, you'll get even more value from InBody scans and in-person coaching, but the system works independently.

Do I need to track macros?

We strongly recommend it, especially in Year 1. Tracking gives you the data to make each phase work. Most people need 2-3 years of consistent tracking before they can intuitively eat at their targets. The Decade Roadmap covers this progression.

What if I'm starting mid-year?

Jump into whatever phase aligns with the current calendar quarter. The system is designed as an annual cycle â€” you'll get the full experience your first January.

Is this a meal plan?

No. The Blueprint gives you macro targets and a structured framework for each phase. You choose what to eat. This is about flexibility and sustainability, not rigid meal plans that fall apart after two weeks.

What if I have a medical condition?

This Blueprint provides general nutrition guidance. It is not medical advice. If you have diabetes, thyroid conditions, eating disorders, or any other medical concern, consult your doctor before starting any nutrition program.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Your subscription renews annually. You can cancel anytime before renewal and keep access through the end of your paid year.
